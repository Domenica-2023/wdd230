Healdsburg Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

By Domenica Baerwald WDD230 Fall 2023

Site Name: Healdsburg Chamber of Commerce

I chose this site name because I am very familiar with the place.

Site Purpose:

The Healdsburg Chamber of Commerce acts as a resource for tourists and other people that are interested in this area. The Chamber of Commerce promotes businesses, publishes and advertises local events and acts a directory of sorts for the Healdsburg community.

Scenarios:

  • What are some places people like to go in Healdsburg?
  • Who is the staff?
  • What upcoming events will be happening?
